Polaroid Memory Flowers

What is the product?

Our Polaroid Memory Flowers are real flowers combined with Polaroid photos to create personalized, heartfelt keepsakes. Each flower is handmade with love and includes a Polaroid photo.

How is the flower made?

We print your photo on a Polaroid and attach it to a carefully preserved real flower, creating a one-of-a-kind keepsake.

Price without customization

The base price for a box with a Polaroid Memory Flower is $16.89, without any customization.
