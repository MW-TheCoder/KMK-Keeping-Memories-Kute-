About Us

We are Kelvyianna, Mariah, and Kamarra, three friends with a passion for creativity and making memories last forever.

Our idea came one cozy night when we were flipping through old Polaroid pictures, reminiscing about all the little moments we had captured. We realized how much joy these simple photos brought us, and we wanted to share that joy with others.

Thus, Keeping Memories Kute was born—a website dedicated to creating personalized, heartwarming keepsakes from your Polaroid memories. We believe that every special moment deserves to be remembered in a unique way!
